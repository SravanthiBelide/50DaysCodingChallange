&#x20;Inferring Performance \& Efficiency

Use your calculated measures to draw conclusions about the scheme's health:



Work Fulfillment Gap: Compare Households\_Demanded vs. Households\_Provided. If the gap is widening over the years (e.g., in Uttar Pradesh or Bihar), it suggests that the administrative machinery is struggling to keep up with rural demand.



Cost Efficiency: Look at Expenditure\_per\_person\_day. If some states have significantly higher costs than the national average without a corresponding increase in Person\_Days\_Generated, it may indicate high material costs or potential leakages.



Yearly Trends: Check for spikes, such as the increase in demand seen in 2020-21. This likely reflects the economic impact of the pandemic and shows how the scheme acted as a safety net.
