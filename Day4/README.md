## &nbsp;     Credit Risk Analysis \& Loan Portfolio Management



##### 1\. Project Objective:





The primary goal is to analyze the bank's consumer loan portfolio to assess credit risk and identify lending trends.

This analysis provides a health report of the current portfolio and forecasts the impact of various economic shifts.



##### 2.Data Preparation:



Data Cleaning: Handled missing values, removed duplicate records, and standardized date and status formats to ensure data integrity.

Data Typing: Verified that all loan amounts are numerical and issue dates follow a consistent date format.

KPI Engineering: Calculated critical metrics including Loan-to-Value (LTV), Debt-to-Income (DTI), Months on Book (loan age), and individual Loan Profit/Loss.



##### 3.Pivot Tables \& Visuals:



Segmented Analysis: Summarized the total funded amounts and loan counts categorized by Loan Status, Loan Grade, and Loan Intent.Trend Analysis: Utilized line charts to track monthly lending activity and funded amounts.



##### 4.What-If Analysis:



Goal Seek: Determined the specific average interest rate required to reach the bank's target profit margin while holding default rates and costs constant.

Scenario Modeling: Compared two economic outlooks:

Optimistic: A 5% decrease in defaults and a 10% interest rate increase.

Pessimistic: A 15% increase in defaults and a 5% interest rate decrease.

Sensitivity Analysis: Created a data table grid to observe how profit fluctuates across varying combinations of default rates (5%–20%) and interest rates (7%–12%).
